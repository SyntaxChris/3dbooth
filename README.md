## 3D SCANNER BOOTH
### Hardware
* Arduino Micro (1)
* Microstep Driver ST-M5045 (2)

### Driver Parameters
##### Scanner Driver
* DIR (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* DIR (DIR) - pin 8 
* PUL (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* PUL (-PUL) - pin 9
* ENA (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* ENA (-ENA) - pin 10
* +A - Red
* -A - Blue
* +B - Green
* -B - Black
* __Amps__ - 2.5A
* __Steps__ - 6400
* __Arduino delay__ - 10 microseconds

##### Table Driver
* DIR (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* DIR (DIR) - pin 5 
* PUL (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* PUL (-PUL) - pin 6
* ENA (+5V) - Arduino +5v
* ENA (-ENA) - pin 7
* +A - Red
* -A - Blue
* +B - Green
* -B - Black
* __Amps__ - 2.5A
* __Steps__ - 6400
* __Arduino delay__ - 10 microseconds
